The Hound and Rat

A meat pie of Redoran origin.

Thought to have originated in the city of Ald'ruhn, this meat pie is a reflection of the city. It's Ingredients are sourced from what would commonly be found in the Ashlands. Vegetables are ill-equiped to grow in the volcanic ash, and so the Dunmer who live there would have to rely on meats primarly. It is advised that outlanders be conservative when using ash salts as it has been known to cause upset stomach.

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 1 pound of ground meat (mixed rat and hound)
  • A hand and a half of cooked saltrice
  • 1 handful of scuttle
  • 1 small kwama egg
  • A pinch of ash salts

Steps

  1. Crumble ground meat into a large saucepan. Add saltrice, and scuttle. Stir in water. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until meat is evenly browned.
  2. Add kwama egg and stir until incorporated.
  3. Ease one pie crust into a 9-inch pie plate. Fill with meat mixture, then top with the remaining pie crust.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is browned, about 25 minutes.
