Wickwheat Toast

While bread in Morrowind is usually baked from saltrice flour, ash yams and wickwheat can also be used to make bread. Wickwheat toast can be topped with scrib jelly for a delicious breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Ash Yams
  • A bushful of Wickwheat
  • Scuttle
  • Scrib Jelly
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pre-prepare your bread:
    1. Grind your whickwheat and ash yams into a powder.
    2. Add water and fold into a dough.
    3. Bake until ready.
    4. Slice bread into even parts.
  2. Coat pan with a pinch of scuttle, and lay sliced bread until browned. Flip and repeat.
  3. Smear a spoonful of scrib jelly on toast.
